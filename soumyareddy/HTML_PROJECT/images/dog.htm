40 Small Dog Breeds That Stay Tiny Forever

These cute pups are pint-size for their whole lives.

Karla Pope and Janece MazeUpdated: Aug 26, 2024

Country Living editors select each product featured. If you buy from a link, we may earn a commission. Why Trust Us?

If you're planning to adopt a canine family member, get ready for plenty of new responsibilities and lots of fun with your sweet companion. The first step you'll have to take includes meeting prospective adoptees at a rescue center and pet shop. You can also opt to browse local classifieds or online at sites like Pet Finder to find an available pet in your community. If you're on the hunting for a small dog, you're in luck. We've compiled a long list of some of the smallest dog breeds.

We're including all the details about 40 of the smallest dog breeds! Find details about these cute and cuddly pooches' physical characteristics and unique behavioral traits. Get ready for an intense crash course in small canine education. Whether you've been considering a spunky chihuahua or contemplating a playful Yorkie, these small dogs are sure to pull at your heartstrings and bring you lots of joyful moments, cozy snuggles, and endless licks of affection. Once you're ready to welcome your new four-legged friend, check out Country Living's adoption tips from experts. And as once you've found the perfect pooch to make your heart skip a beat, browse fun guides to the best girl dog names and best boy dog names to find the rights one for your fur baby. Check out our round-up of 40 of the smallest dog breeds to figure out which forever pint-size pup is right for you.

1

Japanese Chin

These loving pups are perfect for anyone looking for a cute and friendly four-legged friend. Weighing between seven and 11 pounds and measuring between eight and 11 inches tall, they're easy to transport. Plus, they play well with other dogs.

RELATED: Expert Tips on Introducing a New Dog into a Household With Pets

2

Maltese

Their gorgeous, snowy white coats and big, dark eyes will earn them countless compliments. Measuring between seven to nine inches tall and weighing under seven pounds, these loving pets will do almost anything to make you smile.

RELATED: 10 Best Dog Harnesses, Tested by the Pet Lab

3

Pomeranian

These pooches are always on the lookout, and their protective nature makes them great watchdogs. While they're only between six and seven inches tall and weigh between three and seven pounds, they're active—and they make great friends.

RELATED: Dog-o'-Lanterns Ideas for Fall!

4

Chihuahua

Tiny, but feisty, chihuahuas are super energetic. Known as "purse dogs," they're between five and eight inches tall and weigh no more than six pounds. While they're playful, they're surprisingly not known to be the most kid-friendly.

RELATED: The Best Indestructible Dog Toys

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
5

Yorkshire Terrier

Their long, glossy coats make Yorkshire terriers as pretty as they are playful, but don't mistake their striking looks for aloof behavior—they're very protective. They're between seven and eight inches in height and weigh around seven pounds.

RELATED: Most Loyal Guard Dog Breeds to Consider Adding to Your Family

6

Pug

Popular and cute, pugs are a dog fan favorite—and for good reason. Their unique wrinkled brows and expressive faces make them adorable...even when they're being mischievous. These pups stand between 10 and 13 inches tall and weigh between 14 and 18 pounds.

RELATED: Tips to Keep Your Pet Safe at Seasonal Parties

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
7

Shih Tzu

Dubbed "Lion Dog," these homebody pooches are perfect for anyone living in apartments or lacking outdoor space—because while they're playful, they're fine with staying inside. Weighing between nine and 16 pounds and standing between eight and 11 inches tall, these dogs are happy sitting on your lap. Plus, with virtually no shedding, you won't have to worry about constant vacuuming.

RELATED: How Often Should I Wash My Dog?

8

Toy Fox Terrier

Toy fox terriers might be tiny—measuring only between 8.5 and 11.5 inches and weighing between four and nine pounds—but they're big on personality. Plus, they're easy to train.

RELATED: Best Dog Quotes that Express Their Loyalty

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
9

Wire Fox Terrier

Handsome, intelligent, and confident, wire fox terriers make outstanding show dogs. Standing tall at 15.5 inches and weighing between 16 to 18 pounds, their rough and wiry coats make them distinctively adorable. Plus, they live long, full lives.

RELATED: Hiking With Dogs: Everything You Need to Know

10

Volpino Italiano

Not a fan of strangers, these dogs stand between 10.5 and 12 inches tall and weigh eight to 16 pounds (depending on gender). Because they barely bark, they're not the best watchdogs. BUT they make awesome companions for anyone who loves to snuggle.

RELATED: The Best Lap Dog Breeds to Netflix and Chill With

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
11

Affenpinscher

With moderate shedding, you might want to invest in a good vacuum for this breed. Hairy issues aside, they're so darn cute! They've even been likened to beloved "Ewoks," the adorable furry creatures rom Star Wars. If you're in the market for one of your own, they're typically between nine and 11.5 inches and weigh between seven and 10 pounds.

RELATED: This Dog Onesie Keeps Your Pup from Shedding

12

American Hairless Terrier

Standing tall between 12 and 16 inches, American hairless terriers are on the larger side when it comes to small breeds. Weighing between 12 and 16 pounds, they're hefty pups that live long lives. Thanks to their hairless nature, sweeping up fur will be a non-issue.

RELATED: This Heated Bed Will Keep Your Dog Warm — Because Your Pup Gets Cold, Too

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
13

Bichon Frise

Cute and willing to play, bichon frisé dogs are great pets for families. Their glorious hypoallergenic white coats make them the perfect choice for allergy sufferers. Measuring under a foot in height and weighing between 12 to 18 pounds, that signature round haircut really makes this breed stand out.

RELATED: This Tiktok-Favorite Dog Brush Is Only $6 on Amazon

14

Australian Terrier

These balls of energy are sure to keep you on your toes—and because they're extremely vocal and playful in nature, kids will enjoy their company. Their height averages between 10 and 11 inches and they weigh anywhere between 15 and 20 pounds.

RELATED: Dogs Breeds that Make the Best Family Pets

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
15

English Toy Spaniel

These snub-nosed cuties are affectionate and gentle creatures. Standing nine to 10 inches tall and rounding out at around 14 pounds, they're wonderful additions to fun-loving families.

RELATED: Create a Custom Playlist for Your Pet to Keep Them Company While You're at Work

16

Brussels Griffon

Looking for a small pup with a big personality? If so, Brussels griffon dogs are the pets for you. Weighing between eight and 10 pounds, these pooches are not ideal for young kids who tend to roughhouse.

RELATED: A Brussels Griffon Named Newton Won The National Dog Show

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
17

Cairn Terrier

Affectionate towards their owners, cairn terriers are delightful pups to have around. Not topping more than 13 or 14 pounds and standing between 9.5 and 10 inches tall, these dogs are protective and playful.

RELATED: 113 Cute and Funny Dog Instagram Captions

18

Pumi

Energetic and incredibly affectionate, these dogs are nimble-footed with origins from Hungary. Their black corkscrew hair sets them apart from the pack. Because they're known for their need to assess situations, getting them acquainted and comfortable with their surroundings is key. They stand between 15 and 18.5 inches—and while males can weigh up to 30 pounds, female pups land between 22 and 24 pounds.

RELATED: Best Black Dog Names to Suit Your Puppy

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below
19

Havanese

Native to Cuba, these lively pooches are bursting with energy. They get high marks across the board when it comes to friendliness, are super social, and play well with others—including other pups. Their silky coats make stroking hard to resist.

RELATED: 4 Easy Ways to Banish Pet Hair

20

Cocker Spaniel

Their big, welcoming eyes and soft, floppy ears make cocker spaniels one of America's most popular and beloved breeds. Plus, their lush coats come in a variety of colors. Standing about 14 to 15 inches tall and weighing between 20 and 30 pounds (depending on gender), they're short, but sturdy and solid. While you're drooling over how adorable these precious pups are, don't forget to keep a towel handy because they're prone to a little dribble too.
